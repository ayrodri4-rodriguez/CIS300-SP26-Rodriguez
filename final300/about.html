Creative Work and more about me

I first began my journey into coding through designing floor plans. I personally could not handle how confusing the application was and so as a result it is a goal of mine to design a more effective, easy to manage application similar to CAD. In this manner I hope to combine both my interests.

When it comes to my many other interests these include dancing, reading, playing the guitar (or at least attempting to), and fashion!
